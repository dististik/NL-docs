Nisemon Singles Battle Clauses

Click here to see a full list of singles revamps. Click here to return to the rules main page.

Exiled Clause

Any Pokémon tiered in Exiled or above are prohibited.

High Threat Clause

Each team is allowed four pokemon from the High Threat major tier. Only two pokemon from the Sub-Uber minor tier are allowed on a team; each take a High Threat slot.

PKquill Clause

Pokémon are disallowed to have certain moves or abilities if there is a complex ban in place (in the case of exiled megas, their respective mega stones are banned). Pokémon are allowed to have certain moves or abilities if there is a revamp in place.

NFR Clause

Pokémon with an evolution or pre-evolution that has been revamped can also use the changed moves unless otherwise specified. Complex bans do not apply to pre-evolutoins.

Sleep Clause

You may only put one of your opponent's Pokémon to sleep at a time; self-inflicted sleep does not count against this.

Species Clause

Limit one Pokémon under a national dex number on a team.

OHKO Clause

All guaranteed One-Hit Knock Out moves are banned.

Evasion Clause

Minimize is prohibited; one self-inflicted evasion boost is allowed.

Endless Battle Clause

Scenarios in which the only way for the battle to end is if a player forfeits are prohibited.

Baton Pass Clause

Baton Passing self-inflicted stat boosts is prohibited. Drypass (no stat boost Baton Pass) is allowed.

Banned Abilities Clause

The abilities Shadow Tag, Arena Trap, Power Construct, and Moody are all banned.
